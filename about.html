About Us

KageGym was founded in 2023 in the heart of Cork city, KageGym was born out of passion, purpose, and a powerful vision. Started by a group of young entrepreneurs with a shared love for fitness. Our goal was to create a space where everyone from beginers to athletes could train and thrive.

What started as a small fitness has quickly grown inot a dynamic and supportive community, both online and in person. At KageGym, we believe fitness isn't about lifting weights, it's about lifting each other. Thats why we focus not only on strength and conditioning, but also on education, motivation, and balance.
