Balance Diet

A balanced diet is all about giving your body the nutrients it needs to function properly without cutting out any major food groups. It includes the right proportions of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals to support energy, growth and overall health.

What it includes

  • Carbohydrates (e.g. whole grains, fruits, veggies) - your body's main energy source
  • Carbohydrates (e.g. whole grains, fruits, veggies) - your body's main energy source
  • Protein (e.g. lean meat, eggs, tofu, legumes) - for muscle repair and immune support
  • Fats (e.g. nuts, seeds, avocado, olive oil) - helps absorb vitamins and supports brain health
  • Vitamins & Minerals (e.g. leafy greens, dairy, fish) - essential for everything from bone health to mood
  • Water - often overlooked, but critical for hydration and digestion
