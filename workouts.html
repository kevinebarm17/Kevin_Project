Workout at Home

If you have a busy schedule and are not able to attend gym as much as you please, dont worry as our webiste will offer you a basic workourt routine that doesnt require gym equipment that you can use any time. The home workouts we offer you will be aiming for the main components Cardio, core training, and muscle training.

Cardio Workout: Skipping Rope

Jumping rope is an easy plyometric workout that benefits several muscles at the same time. Even with simple movement you're imporving your strength and heart rate. Skipping rope reauires one rope and enough space to use it. Swing the rope aroud your body and continue to do it until you feel a burn sensations.

Cardio Workout: Jumping Jacks

Jumping Jacks are a beginner cardiovascular exercise that a person can perform anywhere. To perform jumping jacks, begin with the feet hip-width apart and arms down. Raise the arms out to the sides, straight in the air while jumping out with the feet apart. Jump back into the starting position, land softly, and repeat the process.

Core Workouts: Russian Twists

Russian twists are an amazing excerice to build core and shoulder strength. It is used among athletes which helps with movements. To perform this excerice you should lift your feet from the floor and your knees bent. Twist your body from side to side with you hands clamped together or use dumbells if present.
